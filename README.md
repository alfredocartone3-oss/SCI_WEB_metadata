Metadata for SCI-WEB project operated within the GRINS dataframe at different scales over 11 well-being domain for the analysis of territorial well-being individuated by Benessere Equo & Sostenibile (BES). Data on AMELIA platform includes:

1) 52 variables at NUTS3/provincial level between 2014 and 2021;
2) 25 variables at LAU/municipal level for 2018 and 2021;
3) 4 well-being indicators at NUTS3/provincial level (indicatori_provinciali_sciweb);
4) 6 well-being indicators at LAU/municipal for 2018 (indicatori_comunali_sciweb_2018);
5) 7 well-being indicators at LAU/municipal for 2021 (indicatori_comunali_sciweb_2021).

All metadata for the SCI-WEB datasets 1) and 2) are expanded in the Report Deliverable_2.1_SCI-WEB_final-2 attached here!
